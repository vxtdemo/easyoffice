Viking has a steel structure factory at Km5, Road 10, Thanh Loi Commune, Vu Ban District, Nam Dinh.

Viking factory is equipped with synchronous lines from automatic billet cutting to finishing painting, meeting all needs related to steel structure production with a high level of specialization.
